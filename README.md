# buynest-e-commerce-performance-analysis-power-bi-dashboard
# Description
This BuyNest E-Commerce Performance Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyse and monitor key business metrics such as sales, profit, customer segments, regional performance, and product category profitability. The purpose of this dashboard is to help business stakeholders and decision-makers gain deep insights into overall company performance, identify profit-generating areas, detect losses, and make data-driven strategic decisions for growth and optimization. This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into meaningful visual insights through interactive charts, KPIs, and filters to evaluate business performance across multiple dimensions including region, category, segment, and time.
# Project Objective
-	To analyse overall sales, profit, and order performance
-	To identify top-performing regions, categories, and sub-categories
-	To track profitability trends and loss-making areas
-	To monitor sales growth and profit growth year-over-year
-	To compare sales target vs achieved performance
-	To analyse customer segments and top customers
-	To provide interactive filters for dynamic business analysis
-	To support management in making data-driven decisions
# Tech Stack Used
-	Power Bi Desktop – Main data visualization platform used for report creation.
-	Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
-	Data Modelling – Relationships established among tables and Date table for time intelligence.
-	DAX (Data Analysis Expression) – Created multiple DAX measures used for calculated memory, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
-	Visualization - KPI Cards, Bar & Column Charts, Donut & Pie Charts, Line Chart (Trend Analysis), Map Visualization, Gauge Chart (Target vs Achieved), Waterfall Chart (Profit Breakdown), Slicers (Year, Region, Segment, Category). 
-	File Format - .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews.
# Business Impact & Key Insights
Overall Performance
-	Total Sales reached $12.6M with $1.5M total profit
-	Profit margin maintained at 12%
-	Over 25K orders processed
-	Strong 52% YoY growth in both sales and profit
Regional Insights
-	Central and North regions generated highest profits
-	Some regions showed lower profitability indicating cost or pricing issues
-	Regional comparison helps identify expansion opportunities
Category Insights
-	Technology category contributes highest profit (~45%)
-	Office supplies provide stable revenue
-	Furniture shows comparatively lower profit margins
Sub-Category Insights
-	High profit: Copiers, Phones, Accessories
-	Loss-making/low profit: Tables and some furniture items
-	Indicates pricing or cost inefficiencies
Customer Insights
-	Consumer segment contributes highest sales (~51%)
-	Top customers generate significant revenue share
-	Opportunity for loyalty & retention strategies
Loss Analysis
-	Loss amount: ~921K
-	13K orders generated losses
-	Indicates discounting or high shipping costs affecting profitability
Trend Analysis
-	Sales peak during certain months (seasonal trends)
-	Profit trend fluctuates indicating cost variations
-	Helps in demand forecasting and inventory planning
# Final Conclusion
This BuyNest E-Commerce dashboard provides a comprehensive view of business performance by integrating sales, profit, customer, and regional data into one interactive platform. The analysis shows that while the company is achieving strong revenue growth and profitability, there are specific sub-categories and regions generating losses that need immediate attention. Technology products and consumer segments drive the majority of profits, making them key growth drivers. Overall, this dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs efficiently, identify trends, and make informed strategic decisions to improve profitability and operational efficiency.
# Recommendations
- Profit Optimization: Reduce discounts on loss-making products. Optimize pricing strategy for furniture category. Focus on high-margin products (Technology).
- Regional Strategy: Invest more in high-performing regions. Improve marketing in low-performing regions. Optimize logistics and shipping costs
- Customer Strategy: Implement loyalty programs for top customers. Target consumer segment with personalized marketing. Improve retention strategies.
- Product Strategy: Promote high-profit sub-categories. Re-evaluate loss-making products. And bundle low-performing products with best sellers.
- Business Intelligence: Use forecasting for demand planning. Set region-wise sales targets. Monitor KPIs regularly through dashboard.
